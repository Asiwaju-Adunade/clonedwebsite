  • Chi Farms Ltd. has been operating in Nigeria since 1986.
  • The farm specializes in poultry breeding, aquaculture, frozen chicken and beef products. Chi Farms produces Parent Stock, commercial pullet & broiler day-old chicks and frozen meat products.
  • The commercial broiler business includes raising, slaughtering and processing broiler birds.
  • Chi Farms also offers innovative diagnostic services through its laboratory facilities at Oluyole, Ibadan. Our customer focus team visits different farms to render training on poultry management and the best poultry farming practices.


  • The main farm is located at Ajanla Village, KM 20, off Ibadan - Lagos Expressway, Ibadan, while the meat processing plant is located at KM 51, off Ibadan - Lagos Expressway, Ogun State. Our cold store and frozen foods distribution centre are located at our corporate head office, Cormart House, Plot A Block 2, Ilupeju Industrial Estate, Apapa - Oworonshoki Expressway, Lagos, Nigeria.
